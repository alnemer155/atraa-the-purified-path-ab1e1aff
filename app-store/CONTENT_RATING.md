# Content Rating — عِتَرَةً (Atraa)

## Apple App Store rating: **4+**
## Google Play / IARC rating: **Everyone**

### Justification
The app contains:
- Religious text (Quran, hadith, duas, adhkar) — all from authoritative,
  mainstream scholarly sources.
- A live broadcast of the Two Holy Mosques (Saudi Broadcasting Authority).

It does **not** contain:
- Violence, gore, or frightening content.
- Sexual or suggestive content.
- Profanity, crude humor, or alcohol/tobacco/drug references.
- Gambling, simulated gambling, or contests.
- Mature/suggestive themes of any kind.
- User-generated content, chat, or unmoderated communication.
- Unrestricted web access.
- Music.

The content is appropriate for all ages.
